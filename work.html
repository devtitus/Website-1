Project
Title 1

INDmoney is an Indian financial app which consolidates the entire family’s finances in one place. It not only enables users to track their financial investments & liabilities but also allows them to directly invest. Users can track all their financial assets across EPFs, stocks, FDs as well as cryptocurrencies and all these services are offered at zero commissions

100M+

Vews On Youtube

200,000+

US Stock Accounts

120,000+

Accounts Funded

We work closely with INDmoney on content ideation and execution not only to generate leads but also open US stock accounts from India. We’ve pushed the idea of 'diversifying one's financial portfolio' via Influencer Marketing and continue amassing a staggering reach along with significant conversion rates for INDmoney.

Objectives

To acquire new Users to open & Fund their US stock accounts on INDmoney App.

Solutions

For INDmoney, our communication has been clear & simple since the start. Our videos revolve around how the US stock markets work, how to invest in it & we’ve also offered free stocks to incentivize audiences to sign up. To improve conversions we've review videos regarding the authenticity of the app & its charges along with demo videos on how to open a US stock account using INDmoney.
